How It Works
ScaleUp helps micro-entrepreneurs like you get the capital you need to scale. It’s simple to start your journey!
Grow Your Business, With Our Community!
STEP 1: Share Your Product Visit a ScaleUp Kiosk. Submit a sample of your best product and tell us your business name and what you need funding for. It’s quick and easy!
STEP 2: We Launch Your Campaign Online Our team takes professional photos of your product and creates a compelling story for your online campaign page on our website.
STEP 3: Get Support from Our Community People browse our website, see your amazing product at the kiosks, and choose to support your business! They can donate, or pre-order your products.
STEP 4: Get Your Capital & Scale Smarter! Once your goal is reached, you receive the capital! Use it to buy materials, upgrade equipment, or expand your reach. ScaleUp helps you grow!
